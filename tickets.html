Terms and conditions

By registering for SpainJS 2013, the registrants agree to the terms and conditions below.

CONFERENCE TICKETS

Entry to the conference on the 5th and 6th of July will only be permitted to persons who have purchased a ticket or sponsorship pack. Every conference ticket includes entry to the event, goody pack, mid-morning breakfast, lunch, and entry to the fiesta on Friday the 5th of July. Tickets are sold on a first come first serve basis. Individual tickets to the fiesta will also be available.

On Thursday the 4th of July, Madrid.js will be organising a series of workshops. Unfortunately, due to space and practical limits, assistence will be limited. Tickets for the workshops will be free, but reservation priority will be given to those who have purchased a conference ticket.
